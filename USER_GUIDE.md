# Planet Rivals — User Guide

## Overview
Planet Rivals is a browser-based wave shooter where you defend Earth from alien mobs. Play solo or team up with friends in multiplayer!

## Controls

| Action | Keyboard | Mobile |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Move | WASD or Arrow Keys | Left joystick |
| Aim | Mouse | Touch right side of screen |
| Shoot | Click or Space | Fire button |
| Switch Weapon | 1-4 | — |
| Open Shop | Q | — |
| Pause | P or Esc | — |
| Team Blast | T (multiplayer) | — |

## Game Modes

### Solo Play
Classic single-player mode. Survive waves, collect Mo$, buy weapons and upgrades.

### Multiplayer (2-4 players)
1. Click **MULTIPLAYER** on the start screen
2. Enter your name
3. **Create a Room** — generates a 4-letter code to share
4. **Join a Room** — enter a friend's room code
5. Host clicks **START GAME** when 2+ players are in the room
6. Play together! Enemies chase the nearest player, Mo$ is shared.

## Enemies

| Type | Shape | Behavior |
|------|-------|----------|
| Normal | Green circle | Standard mob |
| Fast | Pink triangle | Quick, low HP |
| Tank | Orange hexagon | Slow, high HP |
| Exploder | Yellow cluster | Bursts on death |
| Boss | Red star | Appears every 5 waves, very tough |

## Weapons (Buy in Shop)

| Weapon | Cost | Description |
|--------|------|-------------|
| Blaster | Free | Default rapid-fire weapon |
| Shotgun | Mo$ 180 | 5 bullets, wide spread |
| Laser | Mo$ 220 | Ultra-fast piercing beam |
| Freezer | Mo$ 250 | Slows enemies on hit |
| Plasma | Mo$ 300 | Slow but massive damage |
| Rocket | Mo$ 350 | Explodes on impact |

## Team Upgrades (Multiplayer Only)

### Team Blast — Mo$ 500
- **How to use**: Get 2+ players within 200px of each other, then press **T**
- **Effect**: Massive explosion that one-shots tanks/normals and deals 55% HP to bosses
- **Cooldown**: 60 seconds
- **HUD**: Shows cooldown timer and "READY!" indicator at bottom of screen

## Tips
- Protect Earth AND your ship — either dying = lose a life
- You have 5 lives total with respawning
- Earn Mo$ by killing enemies — use it in the shop between waves
- Every 10 waves, you move to a new planet with tougher (but more rewarding) mobs
- In multiplayer, spread out to draw enemies away from Earth, then regroup for Team Blast on tough waves
- Boss waves (every 5th wave) are a great time to use Team Blast
